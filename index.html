👤
Daniel Tejada
Philosopher · Ontologist · Founder

Daniel Tejada is a Philosopher specialising in Ontology.

At 19, he had a spiritual awakening. Not the peaceful kind — the kind that arrives when you realise the life you're living isn't yours. He was deep in addiction, running on conditioning that wasn't his, burying a spark he'd been born with. The awakening didn't fix any of that. It just made it impossible to look away.

That refusal to look away drove him into philosophy, psychology, the study of human behaviour. His neurodivergencies turned into an edge — he started seeing patterns everywhere. In history. In people. In the mechanics of how visions either take root or collapse. He became obsessed with understanding why.

That obsession became a practice. Working with founders across different industries, he kept seeing the same friction: the gap between what someone can clearly see and what they can actually build. He learned how to work that gap. How to meet a person at their actual wiring, remove the blocks, and build systems that hold. His clients don't just make progress — they keep making it long after the work ends.

He calls what he does philosophy because that's what it is. A living framework built from esoteric study, lived experience, and thousands of hours working at the edges of human potential. No templates. No generic advice. Just the truth about where you are, what's in the way, and what to do next.
